# 🌙 Eid Mubarak - An Interactive Gift for Friends

Welcome to your personalized Eid Mubarak digital gift! This interactive experience is designed to bring joy and festive cheer through a series of surprises.

## 🎁 Experience the Journey

Follow these simple steps to enjoy the full celebration:

### 1. The Welcome Start
*   As soon as the page loads, you'll see a festive greeting with animated lanterns and fireworks.
*   **Music:** Most browsers require a click to play music. Tap the **"Start the Eid Journey"** button to begin the experience and activate the beautiful Eid soundtrack.

### 2. Lantern Lighting Ceremony
*   You'll find five traditional lanterns in the night sky.
*   **Action:** Click or tap on each lantern to ignite them one by one.
*   **Goal:** Once all lanterns are lit, a special "Next Surprise" button will appear.

### 3. A Special Surprise Card
*   A golden Eid card will appear addressed "To My Dear Friend."
*   **Action:** Click on the card to watch it flip open and reveal a heartfelt personal message.
*   **Next:** After reading, click the button below the card to proceed.

### 4. Eid Moments & Wishes (Gallery)
*   Sit back and enjoy! This section is an **automatic slideshow** of beautiful memories and wishes.
*   Each picture will appear, stay for a moment, and then **automatically flip** to show a wish on the back.
*   Once all memories have been shown, the final surprise button will be revealed.

### 5. The Grand Gift Reveal
*   A royal red and gold gift box awaits you.
*   **Action:** Tap the gift box to "open" your Eidi.
*   Watch as fireworks and gold rain celebrate your reveal!

### 6. Final Celebration
*   Meet our friendly waving mascot who has a final festive wish for you.
*   Enjoy the confetti and the bright stars as the journey concludes.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Details
*   **Responsive Design:** Works beautifully on both Desktop and Mobile devices.
*   **Music:** Uses a global audio player to ensure the music stays synchronized as you move through the pages.
*   **Animations:** Powered by CSS3 for smooth, high-performance visual effects.
